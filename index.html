About Willow Lake Bed & Breakfast

In 1975, the Marchini family refurbished the Willow Lake Bed and Breakfast, which was originally built in the 1800's. Jim Marchini, the founder, is a hunter, fisherman, trapper and a conversationalist. Willow Lake is located in Copper River Valley, Alaska which has many different activities and local attractions.



BREAKFAST, SERVICES, AND SPECIAL OCCASIONS

At Willow Lake B&B, our breakfast is French-oriented with fruit, cheese crepes, and various croissants. Gluten Free Options Available. We host a "Tea Party" service with high-end custom teas, lady finger sandwiches, and more assortments of sweet finger foods. Our "Tea Party" service can be arranged with occasions such as birthdays, weddings, wedding showers, and others upon request.
